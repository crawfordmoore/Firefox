Firefox Add-ons For Higher Education

This is the base repo for the WRIT 350 technical documentation project.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Our project demonstrates how to use Firefox add-ons in higher education

Firefox has many add-ons that are benefitial in higher education. We believe three specific add-ons, Google Translator, Google Schoalr, and Wired-Marker, are benefitial to both students and professors in higher education.
